Our Mission

We believe in nurturing, guiding and empowering all students to achieve their career dream. Our digital platform guides youth from high-school to Career with curated, customized & comprehensive data to help them match with varied career paths.

Students

With our proprietory MILE Assessment, Students can perform customized assessments of over 40,000+ Career paths, that suit their personality. In addition, the choosen Career is kept aligned to market trends that helps plan and track progress.

Parents

Planning kid’s futures and supporting their growth is one of many responsibilities a Parents owns and we want to make it simple and easy. PathtoCareer, enables parents with knowledge and guided information to support kid’s progress and helps to identify Career paths that are both relatable and comfortable based on their personality.

Financials

Financial planning plays an important role in kid’s education, as investing in oneself is the best investment. But not everyone is equipped with support that helps financial planning, worry not, no matter where you are in the plan or don’t have a plan, no problem, our platform is equipped with all support and service that help in an efficient financial planning for a brighter future
